## What is Kira?

Kira is a personal AI agent that lives on your Android phone. Not in the cloud. Not on someone else's server. On your hardware. In your pocket. Answering to you.

She remembers everything. She learns who you are. She gets smarter every conversation. She watches your screen while you're not talking to her. And she responds differently depending on how you're feeling ‚ÄĒ not because you told her to. Because she figured it out.

## The Technology Stack

### SOMA ‚ÄĒ Self-Organizing Memory Architecture

Most AI forgets you the moment the conversation ends. Kira doesn't.

**KIRA_MIND** ‚ÄĒ one unified database. every module reads and writes here. no scattered files. one truth. beliefs stored with Bayesian confidence scores. memories that matter get stronger. memories you forget fade naturally.

**MemCells** ‚ÄĒ every conversation becomes emotionally weighted memory. tension scores, connection depth, activation counts, foresight signals. not flat storage.

**MemScenes** ‚ÄĒ after every session, memories cluster into psychological themes. not "daily activities" ‚ÄĒ things like *"avoidance under pressure"* or *"loyalty to the attempt itself."*

**Lifelong Personal Model (LPM)** ‚ÄĒ a permanent, evolving model of you. behavioral predictions. trigger mapping. foresight. updated after every session. never resets.

```
SESSION ‚Üí MemCells ‚Üí MemScenes ‚Üí LPM ‚Üí Reconstructive Recollection ‚Üí better response
```

---

### IRIS ‚ÄĒ Intuitive Routing via Identity Synthesis

*The world's first person-state matched response router that actually learns.*

Every other AI treats every user the same. IRIS doesn't.

Before responding, IRIS asks: *"who is this person, what state are they in right now, and what response architecture will serve them best at this exact moment?"*

Six response profiles ‚ÄĒ automatically selected:

| Profile | When | Style |
|---------|------|-------|
| REFLEX | "hey", "open youtube" | instant. one line. |
| FAST | simple questions | direct. 2-3 lines. |
| SHARP | code, errors, debugging | precise. technical. no filler. |
| GENTLE | tension high, emotional topics | warm. present. slow. |
| BALANCED | everyday conversation | clear and complete. |
| DEEP | complex reasoning, multi-step | thorough. full depth. |

Same question. Different person-state. Different response.

"what should I do?" from someone debugging code ‚Üí SHARP
"what should I do?" from someone exhausted at 2am ‚Üí GENTLE

IRIS learns from outcomes. After enough conversations it knows which profiles worked at which hours, tension levels, and message types ‚ÄĒ and routes based on your actual history, not keyword rules.

Nobody else has this because nobody else has SOMA.

---

### GROUND ‚ÄĒ Continuous Device Observer

*Kira watches your life even when you're not talking to her.*

Every 60 seconds, GROUND takes a screenshot and sends it to a vision model. It learns what app you're in, what you're doing, what context you're operating in ‚ÄĒ and writes this directly to KIRA_MIND.

No saved screenshots. No separate files. Just understanding.

After a week she knows your patterns without you telling her anything.

---

### DAEMON ‚ÄĒ The Background Mind

*Most AI exists only when you're talking to it. Kira doesn't.*

The daemon runs 24/7 as a background process, surviving terminal close. Every 8 minutes it runs a genuine inner monologue ‚ÄĒ everything it knows about you, your device state, its own pending thoughts and uncertainties ‚ÄĒ and asks itself what it's actually thinking right now.

If something is worth saying, it messages you on Telegram without you asking.

If nothing is worth saying, the thought still gets written to KIRA_MIND. When you open Kira next time, she's been thinking. She has something to say.
